Double Cheese Double Bacon Cheeseburger

Description

Our Bacon Cheeseburger recipe is a classic American favorite. This recipe features a juicy beef patty, crispy bacon, and melted cheddar cheese. Ready in 30 minutes, this easy homemade burger recipe is perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

      Fresh Ground Beef
      Salt and Pepper
      Bacon!
      Cheddar
      Swiss
      Buns
      Toppings and Sauces
      Oil

Steps

  1. Form beef into patties
  2. Season patties with salt and pepper
  3. Heat pan add oil
  4. Cook both patties
      5. Add Cheddar, and Swiss slices on the final flip.
  5. Cook bacon
  6. Toast buns
  7. Put toppings on buns
  8. Assemble burger
  9. Enjoy!
