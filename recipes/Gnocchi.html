Gnocchi alla Sorrentina

Description

Our Gnocchi alla Sorrentina recipe is a classic Italian dish. This recipe features soft potato gnocchi, fresh tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella cheese. Ready in 45 minutes, this easy homemade gnocchi recipe is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

      Extra-Virgin olive oil
      Garlic
      Chili flakes
      Tomatoes puree
      Salt
      Fresh Basil
      Gnocchi
      Mozzarella
      Parmesan
      Asiago

Steps

  1. Add tomato puree
  2. Boil puree, and season
      3. Garlic, chili flakes, and basil.
  3. Cook the gnocchi
  4. Layer the dish
  5. Add cheese
  6. Plate, garnish, and enjoy!
